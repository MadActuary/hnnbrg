About Me

Hello, I’m Lasse Henneberg — an actuary based in Copenhagen with four years of experience in actuarial science. I began my career working three years in health insurance modelling at Danica. Today, I work at AP Pension, focusing on the Loyalty Program and commercial analysis. In my daily work I use git, R, and SQL.

I hold an MSc in Actuarial Science from the University of Copenhagen, supplemented by an advanced fixed income course. My key strengths include statistical modeling and clear business communication. I have recently co-authored an article with my wife.
